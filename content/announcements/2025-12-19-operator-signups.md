If you are interested in booking a slot for the primary or secondary radio rooms to operate for Winter Field Day 2026 Jan 24-25th please let us know by signing up with the signup genius below:

<https://www.signupgenius.com/go/70A0C4EAAAF2AA7FD0-61345046-winter>

Those who signs up will have priority over other users during Winter Field Day 2026. Anyone who does not sign up will be able to operate only on a first come first serve basis!

So, if you would like to make sure that your time at the club for WFD 2026 is spent contesting without interruption please make an effort to sign up for some dedicated time slots. During prime time 11:00AM - 4PM Saturday we ask that you only sign up for one hour block at a time to allow for many people to play. No limit on additional slots outside primetime. You wanna book midnight to 6AM Sunday you go nuts and do it!

A couple days before WFD we send out reminders and attempt to normalize the schedule for conflicts. It is imperative that you leave a valid email when signing up.

We will try to have two HF stations and a single VHF station operating. The second HF station is contingent on the availability of band pass filters. ( We have burned through two sets since the last Field Day operation and are looking for replacements / donations.)

Regards and thanks for you participation. It should be a fun weekend at the clubhouse!
