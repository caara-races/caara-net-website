Hello CAARAMAILERs and CAARA members,

This is a reminder that we will be operating a special event station at the Crane Estate on Castle Hill in Ipswich, MA, next Tuesday and Wednesday.

Setup begins at 8:30 AM and tear-down will be around 4:00 PM both days. Volunteers are welcome to help. Please let me know if you have any questions at <bhockle@gmail.com>.

In the mornings, we will host 50 campers from the Trustees' Summer Quest Program to introduce them to amateur radio and the Cape Ann Amateur Radio Association. If you would prefer dedicated operational time on our two stations, please plan to join us in the afternoon.

Please note that it is peak [greenhead] season, so bug repellent is highly recommended. Please dress appropriately and bring water to stay hydrated.

Those of you who cannot make it in person please listen for us and make a contact! Special event QSL cards are available to the first 100 contacts.

Best regards,
Brandon Hockle NQ1W
