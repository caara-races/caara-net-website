Dear Members,

Please help the Cape Ann Amateur Radio Association by taking a minute to ensure that we have accurate and updated contact information for you on our membership rolls.

We also want to verify that all users can access the member area of the club's website. To do this, we ask that all members please verify their details (Name, Callsign, Class, and most importantly email address) here: <https://members.caara.net/content/members/>

Thank you for helping us keep our records up to date. Your current email address on record in our members area is the only way we can ensure you receive membership renewal information, the newsletter, and important notices. If any of your info needs updating, please use our google form in order to report a discrepancy: CAARA Digital Committee Problem Reporting Form

When reporting the problem specify the website and the membership list url and what information needs updating. The Digital Committee requests that If there is nothing to change regarding your email or information, please do not submit a problem report.

Thank you so much for your membership and for helping CAARA keep an up to date and accurate member roll!

Best regards,
Brandon Hockle (NQ1W)
