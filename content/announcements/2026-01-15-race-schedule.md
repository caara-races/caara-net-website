Greetings CAARA Members,

I hope you are off to a great start on the new year. The 2026
CAARA-YuKanRun race schedule has been published and our
[Race Volunteer Web Site](https://sites.google.com/view/caararaces/home)
has been updated with the new races, dates, times, and locations.

<!-- more -->

This is the first call for volunteers for the coming race season. There are
many spots to fill so I ask you to sign up for as many races as you can, as
early as you can.

Please visit the [web site](https://sites.google.com/view/caararaces/home),
pick your desired checkpoints, and email me to sign up for races.

[Email Kevin, K1KL](mailto:kevinlyonsk1kl@gmail.com) with your choices.

Three individuals will be in the role of Net Controller for the new season-
WA1ESU, N1LKS, and K1KL. We would like to train one or two additional hams
for Net Control. Let me know if you are interested and we will set up a Net
Control “shadow” training for you.

If you have never participated as a race volunteer before, have no fear.
There is a training manual on the website and we can arrange a telephone
mini-training before your first race. Hop on board!
