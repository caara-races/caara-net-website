CAARA is launching an exciting new initiative designed to get our newly
licensed HAMs on the air and to encourage unlicensed enthusiasts to join our
hobby. To ensure the success of this program, we are forming the "CAARA Elmer
Corp," and I am writing to personally ask for your support.

<!-- more -->

We are looking for experienced operators who are willing to share their
knowledge and passion. As an Elmer, you will play a vital role in mentoring
newcomers, offering technical guidance, and helping them build the confidence
needed to navigate the airwaves. Whether you can offer a few hours a month or
provide more consistent guidance, your contribution will be invaluable to the
future of our community and will help us strengthen the amateur radio hobby at
large.

If you are interested in joining the CAARA Elmer Corp or have any questions
about the program, please direct your inquiries to <info@caara.net>. We are also
interested in knowing if you already have a mentoring relationship with a
current member. I want to know for sure who is already in it to win it!

Thank you for your continued dedication to our club and for helping us support
the next generation of HAMs and CAARA members.
