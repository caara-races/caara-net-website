I am writing to solicit your participation this Monday, March 30th, at 12:30 PM for a demonstration to the members of the Gloucester Council on Aging at the Rose Baker Senior Center in Downtown Gloucester.

<!-- more -->

Thomas, {% callsign "AA1TS" %}, and myself will be providing a live demo and speaking with their members about how to get involved with ham radio. Thank you to all the members who have already signed up to attend in person. Hopefully, we will add some new members to our club as a result of this outreach.
