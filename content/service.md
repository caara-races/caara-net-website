## Race & event support

CAARA is a ARRL recognized Special Service Club and we strive to live up to that honor. CAARA provides runner safety nets for half marathons in the Cape Ann region throughout the year. For more information or to get involved, visit the [race support website].

[race support website]: https://sites.google.com/view/caararaces/home

## Emergency communication services

Part of our mission is to provide emergency communication services to Gloucester and surrounding communities. For more information, please see our [emergency operations handbook].
