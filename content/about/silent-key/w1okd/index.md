CAARA member **William "Bill" Canty W1OKD**, age 97, passed away peacefully
Saturday, November 19th, 2022, surrounded by family. Bill was active in his
community as a member of the Manchester Yacht Club, the Manchester Lions
Club, and as a parishioner and lay minister at Sacred Heart Parish.

During World War II he served in the US Navy for two years as a Radio
Technician at the Naval Research Laboratory in Washington, D.C. Bill was a
long-time member of the Cape Ann Amateur Radio Association.
