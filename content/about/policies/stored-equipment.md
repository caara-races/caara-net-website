---
title: Equipment Being Stored at any CAARA Facility
tags: policy
---

The CAARA Board of Directors has voted that the Board itself, any Officers
or Directors of the Board, The Cape Ann Amateur Radio Association as an
entity in itself or any member of CAARA will not and can not be held
financially responsible for any damage to, or loss of, any amateur
radio-related equipment while that equipment is being stored at the
clubhouse or while being used at a club sponsored event.

All persons who bring or store their own personal equipment at the
clubhouse on 6 Stanwood Street or to a club sponsored event do so at their
own risk.
