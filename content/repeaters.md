CAARA operates two repeaters.

## 2M VHF Repeater

| Frequency | Offset | Tone  |
| --------- | ------ | ----- |
| 145.130   | -      | 107.2 |

The antenna is located on the AT&T tower in the [Blackburn Industrial Complex] in Gloucester. It has an average effective radius of 60 miles, and serves eastern Massachusetts, Cape Cod, Rhode Island, southern New Hampshire, and maritime mobile stations.

## 70CM UHF Repeater

| Frequency | Offset | Tone  |
| --------- | ------ | ----- |
| 443.700   | +      | 107.2 |

This is a digital Fusion repeater located on the AT&T tower in the [Blackburn Industrial Complex], with Cape Ann coverage.
