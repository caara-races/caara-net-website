CAARA operates two repeaters:

- The W1GLO 2m repeater on 145.130 Mhz (- offset, 107.2 ctcss tone). The antenna is located on the AT&T tower in the Blackburn Industrial Complex in Gloucester. It has an average effective radius of 60 miles, and serves eastern Massachusetts, Cape Cod, Rhode Island, southern New Hampshire, and maritime mobile stations.

- The W1GLO UHF repeater on 443.700 (+ offset, 107.2 ctcss tone). This is a digital Fusion repeater located on the AT&T tower in the Blackburn Industrial Complex, with Cape Ann coverage. Both are available for club use.
